History

In 1989, ASGC started as a civil works contractor in Dubai, the vision of Emad Azmy, Mohamad Saif Bin Shafar and Mohamed Al Sayyah. In the three decades since, ASGC has become a leading construction conglomerate. It has been a journey full of passion determination, challenges, achievements, dedication, and talent.
